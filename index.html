Model notes. Heading error is modelled as gyro bias-instability (grows ∝t) RSS-combined with angle random walk (grows ∝√t); that heading error integrates into cross-track position error. Along-track error follows the same structure from accelerometer bias-instability and velocity random walk. Total position error is the RSS of cross-track and along-track terms. This is a first-order, worst-case dead-reckoning model: it assumes a static residual bias between fixes, no in-run bias re-estimation or Kalman filtering, no Schuler-loop dynamics, and no sensor cross-coupling or vehicle motion effects (sculling, vibration rectification). Treat outputs as order-of-magnitude sizing intuition for comparing IMU grades and fix intervals — not a certified navigation-error budget.
